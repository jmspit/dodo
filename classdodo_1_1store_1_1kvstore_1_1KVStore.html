enum dodo::store::kvstore::KVStore::ShareMode
strong

The share mode of the KVStore.

Enumerator
Private 

Do not share outside process (but sharing between threads possible).

Shared 

Share with other processes.

Clustered 

Share with other processes on other nodes (which requires a cluster filesystem).
